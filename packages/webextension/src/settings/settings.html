RecipeSage Browser Extension How-To


This is a partial tutorial. To see the complete tutorial, click here.
To start, click the RecipeSage icon next to the address bar. If you're not logged in, a popup will prompt you to enter your RecipeSage username and password.

After you've logged in, you'll be able to click the RecipeSage icon to open the snipping tool.
The snipping tool allows you to quickly snip recipe details from the current webpack and save it directly to your account.


To snip a recipe, first select some text on the page. Then press the scissors icon next to one of the fields listed.
The text you've selected on the page will be copied directly to the corresponding recipe field.
After snipping a field, you are free to edit it.
To snip a recipe image, right click on an image in the webpage and select the "Snip Image" option.
When you are finished snipping all of the fields desired, click the save button. A new recipe will be created in your RecipeSage account.
